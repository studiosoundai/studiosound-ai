Looking for quick answers?

Everything you need to know before reaching out.

How do I generate cover art?
Go to your dashboard, click Cover Art Generator, enter your song title, pick your genre and mood, describe your vision, and hit Generate. You'll get two unique AI versions in under 30 seconds. Pick your favorite and download instantly — no watermark on paid plans.
Who owns the content I generate?
You do — 100%. Everything you generate on StudioSound.ai belongs to you. Use it commercially, upload to DSPs, print on merch, run ads — no restrictions whatsoever. Your art is yours the moment it's generated.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to Settings in your dashboard, click Billing, and click Cancel Subscription. You keep full access until the end of your current billing period. No cancellation fees, no questions asked.
What file size is the cover art?
Every cover art is automatically sized to 3000×3000 pixels JPEG — the exact standard required by Spotify, Apple Music, Tidal, Amazon Music, and most major DSPs. Download and upload directly without any resizing needed.
Is there a free plan?
Yes — completely free, no credit card needed. The free plan includes 3 cover arts, 1 release plan, and 2 merch mockups every month. Upgrade to Pro for $9.99/month to unlock unlimited generations.
How does the Release Planner work?
Enter your project name, release date, genre, city, and tell us about your project. Our AI builds a personalized 30-day marketing roadmap with specific dates, post times optimized for your timezone, playlist pitch contacts, pre-save campaign steps, and press outreach timeline.
Can I upload my own photo for cover art?
Yes! Click "Add Image" in the Cover Art Generator and upload a photo of yourself or any reference image. Our AI will incorporate you directly into the cover art based on your description. Put yourself on your own label-quality cover art in seconds.
What is the merch mockup tool?
Upload your cover art or design, pick a product type (t-shirt, hoodie, hat, poster, phone case, and more), describe what you want, and our AI generates a real photorealistic mockup with your art on the product. We also show you a full cost breakdown so you know exactly what it costs to sell merch.
What AI models power StudioSound.ai?
StudioSound.ai is partnered with the world's leading AI companies, and we manage all of that technology behind the scenes so you never have to. Every time you generate, you get two unique versions from two different AI partners side by side — pick the one that fits your vision. As better models are released, we upgrade automatically. You always create with the best, without ever thinking about what's under the hood.
