FAQ

Common questions

Do I need design or technical experience?
Not at all. StudioSound.ai is built for artists, not designers or developers. If you can type a song title and pick a mood, you can generate professional cover art in under 10 seconds.
Who owns the content I generate?
You do. Everything you generate on StudioSound.ai belongs to you. Use it commercially, upload to DSPs, print on merch, and run ads with it — no restrictions.
Is the cover art actually sized correctly for streaming platforms?
Yes. Every cover art is automatically sized to 3000×3000 pixels JPEG — the standard required by Spotify, Apple Music, Tidal, and most DSPs.
Is StudioSound.ai connected to Stop One Distribution?
Yes. StudioSound.ai is built by the team behind Stop One Distribution, giving artists in the Stop One family direct access and exclusive early features before public launch.
Can I cancel my subscription at any time?
Absolutely. No long-term contracts, no cancellation fees. Cancel anytime from your account settings and keep access until the end of your billing period.
