Questions, answered
Everything you need to know before subscribing.
What's included in the free plan?
The free plan gives you 3 cover arts, 1 release plan, 2 merch mockups, and a watermarked lyric video every month. No credit card needed — ever.
What is AI video clipping?
StudioSound's AI Clipping tool watches your long videos — performances, studio sessions, music videos, live streams — finds the best moments automatically, and turns them into short clips sized for TikTok, Reels, and YouTube Shorts. Captions added automatically. Perfect for artists and streamers who want daily content without daily editing.
Do I own the content I generate?
Yes. 100%. Everything you generate on StudioSound.ai belongs to you. Use it commercially, upload to DSPs, print on merch, run ads — no restrictions whatsoever.
What's the difference between monthly and yearly?
Monthly gives you full flexibility. Yearly saves you 30% — nearly 4 months free. Both plans include all the same features. You can switch anytime.
Can I cancel at any time?
Absolutely. No long-term contracts, no cancellation fees. Cancel anytime from your account settings and keep full access until the end of your billing period.
Is the cover art sized correctly for streaming platforms?
Yes. Every cover art is automatically sized to 3000×3000 pixels JPEG — the exact standard required by Spotify, Apple Music, Tidal, and most major DSPs.
