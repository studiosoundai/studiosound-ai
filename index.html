FAQ

Common questions

Do I need design or technical experience?
Not at all. StudioSound.ai is built for artists, not designers or developers. If you can type a song title and pick a mood, you can generate professional cover art in under 30 seconds.
Who owns the content I generate?
You do — 100%. Everything you generate belongs to you. Use it commercially, upload to DSPs, print on merch, run ads — no restrictions whatsoever.
Is the cover art sized correctly for streaming platforms?
Yes. Every cover art is automatically sized to 3000×3000 pixels JPEG — the exact standard required by Spotify, Apple Music, Tidal, and most DSPs.
Can I upload my own photo for cover art?
Yes. Click "Add Image" in the Cover Art Generator and upload a photo of yourself. Our AI will incorporate you directly into the cover art based on your description.
Can I cancel my subscription at any time?
Absolutely. No long-term contracts, no cancellation fees. Cancel anytime from your account settings and keep access until the end of your billing period.
What makes StudioSound.ai different from other AI tools?
StudioSound.ai is the only platform built exclusively for independent music artists. We also generate two unique AI versions side by side so you can compare and pick the best one — no other music platform does this.
